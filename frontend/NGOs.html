NGO Partnership Program

Join hands with Nyaya Kavach to create a stronger, more inclusive justice delivery system. Together, we can ensure no victim is left without support and every case receives the attention it deserves.

Register Your NGO

Partnership Requirements

  • ✓ Registered NGO under Section 8 or 12A
  • ✓ Minimum 2 years of operational experience
  • ✓ Focus on legal aid, victim support, or social justice
  • ✓ Valid FCRA registration (if applicable)
  • ✓ Qualified legal professionals on staff

Documents Required

  • • NGO Registration Certificate
  • • 12A/80G Certification
  • • Audited Financial Statements
  • • Board Resolution
  • • PAN & GST Registration

Services You Can Provide

⚖️

Legal Aid Services

Provide free legal consultation and representation to victims who cannot afford legal services.

🤝

Victim Support

Offer emotional support, counseling, and rehabilitation services to crime victims.

🏠

Shelter Services

Provide temporary shelter and safe housing for victims of domestic violence and trafficking.

🎓

Legal Awareness

Conduct workshops and seminars to educate communities about their legal rights and procedures.

👥

Community Mediation

Facilitate alternative dispute resolution and community-based justice mechanisms.

📞

Helpline Services

Operate 24/7 helplines for immediate assistance and crisis intervention.

Partnership Benefits

  • 🎯

    Direct Case Referrals

    Receive direct referrals from the Nyaya Kavach system for cases that require NGO intervention and support services.

  • 💰

    Funding Opportunities

    Access government funding schemes and grants specifically allocated for partner NGOs in the justice delivery ecosystem.

  • 📊

    Data & Analytics

    Access comprehensive data and analytics to measure impact, track outcomes, and improve service delivery.

  • 🏆

    Recognition & Awards

    Official recognition from government agencies and opportunities for awards and certifications for outstanding service.

  • 🎓

    Training & Capacity Building

    Regular training programs, workshops, and capacity building sessions to enhance your team's skills and knowledge.

  • 🌐

    Network Access

    Connect with other NGOs, legal professionals, and government agencies to build a stronger support network.
